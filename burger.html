Classic Juicy Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onions
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayo

Instructions:

  1. Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  2. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side until desired doneness.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or skillet.
  5. Assemble your burger with lettuce, tomato, onions, cheese, and your favorite condiments.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your classic burger!

Nutritional Information (optional):

Approximately 350-400 calories per burger, depending on toppings and condiments.
