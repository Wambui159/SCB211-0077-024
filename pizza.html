Delicious Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, bell peppers, olives, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Mix warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  3. Combine flour and salt in a bowl, then add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Nutritional Information (optional):

Approximately 250-300 calories per slice, depending on toppings.
